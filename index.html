Land Use Classification from Satellite Imagery

Rule-based land cover classification pipeline using Sentinel-2 multispectral bands. Computes NDVI, NDWI and BRI spectral indices to map Dense Vegetation, Sparse Vegetation, Water, Urban and Bare Soil classes over large geographic areas. Exports georeferenced GeoTIFF results with per-class statistics.
